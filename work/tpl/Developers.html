BIOMIO for Developers


Biomio is a Password-optional, Risk-based Multi-factor Authentication service that can be connected to any system, application, or device.


To authenticate a person or a resource, BIOMIO makes use of variety of biometric and contextual information, driven by customizable, differentiated policies and built-in AI algorithms.


Biomio MFA verification (and identification) can work “out-of-band” via a separate mobile application or “in-band” via an appropriate client.



Integration Methods:



Open ID Connect

BIOMIO acts as an OpenID Provider offering secure, server-based, Auth2.0 integration to the application developers.
