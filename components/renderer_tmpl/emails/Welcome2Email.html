About BIOMIO Email Protector:

BIOMIO created an Email Protector in order to assist people and organizations keep their communication private and secure. The email is encrypted end-to-end, preventing any snooping either on the servers, in sender or receiver's email clients, in the browser, or on the mobile device. Email Protector is based on PGP encryption combined with BIOMIO password-optional Multi-factor authentication platform.
