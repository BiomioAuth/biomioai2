    Why Sidekick?






    Ransomware is becoming a very big problem for both individuals and corporates alike. The attacker gets into the victim's system either through a virus or directly and encrypts all the user files with their key. The only way to recover those files is to pay the attacker a ransom to decrypt them.

    Biomio Sidekick Features:

    Your files are encrypted the right way!

    Most modern encryption systems encrypt the entire drive with keys stored on the host PC - this gives anyone or anything (like a virus) that has access to your drive an ability to see or manipulate your files.

    Furthermore, if the keys are lost, the files can no longer be decrypted. With Biomio Sidekick, the keys stored on the Sidekick itself and are backed up to your phone, protected by Biomio frictionless and reliable authentication.

    Built-in Defense from Ransom-ware

    .. or other types of malware that messes with your files by re-authenticating (re- confirming) dangerous operations:

    File deletes
    File modifications
    File moves


    Authentication is quick and easy by simply touching the fingerprint sensor or via Biomio authenticator application on the phone.

    Room expander for your phone

    By enabling you to securely back up pictures and videos while freeing up space on your mobile device.

    Automatic filter

    Limit the file types, their size or the owner of the file. Verify files copied onto the media by validating them with built-in file validators.

    Enterprise-ready

    With configurable policies covering authentication, file restrictions, data tokenization, VPN, remote data wiping, and more. We integrate ability to use enterprise PKI with Biomio Sidekicks.
