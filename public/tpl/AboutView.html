About BIOMIO

Today we live in a world of hundreds of logins and passwords, PIN codes, keys, key cards, id's, etc.. We log in multiple times a day, scrambling to remember the account we used, only to reset the password yet again, and again, and again. This proliferation of digital accounts is unmanageable and creates a huge hurdle for both people and for organizations.

BIOMIO is a “friendly security” software company that makes it its mission to provide user-friendly and effective solutions for user authentication, transitioning away from passwords, keys, key cards, and other imperfect and cumbersome access verification methods of the past, to the methods that .

At the core of Biomio platform is a flexible, standards-compliant authentication platform with a dynamic, cross-platform, password-optional multi-factor authenticator. Biomio also provides connectors to single or federated ID stores or can itself take on the role of identity management provider. Rich administrator tools are provided as part of the solution to accommodate for highly granular, differentiated access control and risk-based, user-choice sensitive authentication.
